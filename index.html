Hi! I’m a Chicago-based creator of data visualizations and infographics. I enjoy math, art, and writing, and I’m happy to have crafted a career at the intersection of all three. These days, I work for a global consulting firm where I collaborate with business and economics researchers. My background is in both explanatory journalism and business-school academia, and along the way, I developed some expertise in typography, graphic design, art direction, and website development. I especially like projects that span printed and digital platforms, in both interactive and static formats.
